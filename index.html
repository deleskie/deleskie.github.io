About

James DeLeskie

I cut my teeth building some of the first ISPs in Canada and Ireland, engineered the largest routers of their era at Avici, and spearheaded global DDoS defense efforts at Tata Communications. I founded Mimir Networks to democratize network security and later served as a consulting engineer at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure.

These days, I help organizations protect and automate their networks while mentoring the next generation of engineers. When I'm not designing systems, you’ll find me at a heavy‑metal concert—bringing the same passion to music that I do to resilience.
