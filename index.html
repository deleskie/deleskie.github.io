About Me

With more than 25 years in networking and security, I’ve helped build some of the earliest ISPs in Canada and Ireland, engineered the world’s largest router at Avici Systems, and developed global DDoS detection platforms for Tata Communications. Today, I’m a consulting member of technical staff at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, where I design scalable defenses against cyber‑threats and bring heavy‑metal energy to network engineering.

I founded Heimdall Networks and later Mimir Networks, delivering anomaly‑detection services to SMEs. I speak regularly at NANOG, CanSecWest, AtlSecCon and other conferences, sharing candid insights on BGP filtering, zero‑trust networks and DDoS mitigation.
