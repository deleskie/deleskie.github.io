Philosophy

The tattoo is not decoration placed on the body. It becomes part of the body.

Daemon’s work is described through energy, flow, sacred geometry, ancient body art traditions, dotwork, and the client’s own “soulprint.” The site should not feel like a standard tattoo booking page. It needs to feel deliberate, intense, and personal.

The visual system here uses coded fields, lines, circles, and pressure points as placeholders for his actual images. Once approved work is available, those images become the center of gravity.

Process

Consultation, setting, session, reflection.

  1. 01 Pre-tattoo consultation

    Time for story, placement, intention, body movement, scale, and the emotional weight of the mark.

  2. 02 Set and setting

    The room, music, rhythm, breath, and trust shape the experience as much as the design.

  3. 03 Dot by dot endurance

    Large work can become a focused ritual of time, attention, repetition, and controlled intensity.

  4. 04 Aftercare and integration

    The finished tattoo is treated as a marker of experience, not just the end of an appointment.
