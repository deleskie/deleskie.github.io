About

More Than Just Music

TC Audio Productions has been the soundtrack to Atlantic Canada's most memorable weddings since 1992. With decades of experience and a sharp feel for the room, Tommy brings the energy, atmosphere, and timing that turn a solid night into a lasting memory. From the first dance to the final track, the flow is handled.
